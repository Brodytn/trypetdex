How it works

From curiosity to confident ownership in under 4 minutes.

No overwhelming research. No inconsistent staff advice. Just a clear, guided path to the right pet — every time.

1
Species & lifestyle quiz
Customers choose their species of interest, then answer tailored questions specific to that pet type. Fully tap-to-choose — no typing.
2
Personalized pet match
PetDex surfaces the best-fit animal with a plain-English explanation of why it matches — and a "not for me" option if they want to refine further.
3
Honest cost breakdown
Year-one and monthly ongoing costs, itemized and sourced from ASPCA and veterinary care guides. No surprises after the purchase.
4
Cart + QR handoff
A QR code and "Add to Cart" button open a pre-built starter cart with every recommended item mapped to your store's catalog.
